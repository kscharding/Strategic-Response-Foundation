ID Automator Action Pack

By Automated Workflows, LLC

Open the Mac App Store to buy and download apps.

Description

This action pack extends the power of Automator with 20 actions for performing interacting with Adobe InDesign CS4 or CS5 in Mac OS X!

* Add Pages to InDesign Documents
* AutomatedWorkflows-InDesign
* Close InDesign Documents
* Export InDesign Documents to PDF
* Find and Change Text in InDesign Documents
* Get All Opened InDesign Documents
* Get Front InDesign Document
* Get Linked Images from InDesign Documents
* Get Names of InDesign Documents
* Get Paths of InDesign Documents
* Get Text from InDesign Documents
* Merge InDesign Documents
* New InDesign Document
* Open Documents in InDesign
* Package InDesign Documents
* Place Images in InDesign Document
* Place Text in InDesign Document
* Print InDesign Documents
* Save InDesign Documents
* Save InDesign Documents as Single Page Documents
* Update Modified Links in InDesign Documents

APPLICATION REQUIREMENTS

Please note that you must own a copy of Adobe InDesign CS4 or CS5/CS5.5 installed in order to use these Automator actions.

INSTALLATION

1) Download the action pack from the Mac App Store
2) Launch the action pack application (this is necessary to register the actions with Automator, and must be done whenever an updated action pack is downloaded and installed)
3) Relaunch Automator

GETTING STARTED

If you're new to Automator, check out the video podcast series, Mac Automation Made Simple, available online http://www.automatedworkflows.com/tips/videos/ and in iTunes. This covers a lot of the basics.

The user guide for the actions is accessible from the Help menu in the main action pack, or may be downloaded here: http://www.automatedworkflows.com/files/docs/products/AW_Automator_Actions_Lio-
n.pdf

Sample workflows for this action pack are available for download here: http://www.automatedworkflows.com/forum/viewforum.php?f=45

FOR PREVIOUS USERS OF OUR AUTOMATOR ACTION PACKS

Due to changes in some actions and Automator, you may need to reconstruct your existing workflows. To do this, simply open the workflows and replace the previous versions of our actions with the updated versions.
